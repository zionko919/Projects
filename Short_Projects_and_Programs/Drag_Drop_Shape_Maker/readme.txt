Instructions:
1. Open index.html on to your browser.
2. Click on shape button from the bottom to draw shape you want.
3. Using your mouse, click and drag inside the canvas. You will see a line.
4. The line is a guide for shape's size and location draw your shape anywhere inside the canvas
